Technology Trade Contract

This Technology Trade Contract ("Contract") is made and entered into on this [Date] (the "Effective Date") by and between [Your Company Name], hereinafter referred to as the "Seller," and [Client's Company Name], hereinafter referred to as the "Buyer," collectively referred to as the "Parties."

Overview:
1.1 The Seller agrees to sell and deliver the technology products listed in Exhibit A (the "Products") to the Buyer.
1.2 The Buyer agrees to purchase the Products from the Seller, as specified in Exhibit A, subject to the terms and conditions outlined in this Contract.

Product Description:
2.1 The Products to be traded are described in detail in Exhibit A, including specifications, quantities, and any additional relevant details.
2.2 The Seller warrants that the Products conform to the specifications provided and will be free from defects at the time of delivery.

Purchase Price and Payment:
3.1 The Buyer agrees to pay the purchase price as stated in Exhibit A, including any applicable taxes or fees.
3.2 Payment terms shall be outlined in Exhibit A, including the payment schedule, methods of payment, and any late payment penalties.

Delivery:
4.1 The Seller shall deliver the Products to the Buyer's specified location as agreed upon in Exhibit A.
4.2 Delivery dates and times shall be mutually agreed upon by the Parties and documented in writing.
4.3 The Buyer shall inspect the Products upon receipt and notify the Seller of any discrepancies or damages within [number] days of delivery.

Title and Risk of Loss:
5.1 Title to the Products shall transfer from the Seller to the Buyer upon full payment of the purchase price.
5.2 Risk of loss or damage to the Products shall transfer to the Buyer upon delivery.

Intellectual Property:
6.1 The Seller warrants that it has the necessary rights and permissions to sell the Products and that the Products do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights.
6.2 The Buyer agrees not to reproduce, distribute, or modify the Products without obtaining prior written consent from the Seller.

Limitation of Liability:
7.1 In no event shall either Party be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages arising out of or related to this Contract.
7.2 The total liability of either Party under this Contract shall not exceed the total purchase price paid or payable for the Products.

Confidentiality:
8.1 Both Parties agree to keep all non-public information obtained during the course of this Contract confidential.
8.2 This confidentiality obligation shall survive the termination or expiration of this Contract.

Governing Law and Dispute Resolution:
9.1 This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [State/Country].
9.2 Any disputes arising out of or related to this Contract shall be resolved through good-faith negotiations between the Parties.
9.3 If the Parties are unable to reach a resolution, the dispute shall be submitted to binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of [Arbitration Institution/Body].

Entire Agreement:
10.1 This Contract, including all attached exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior discussions, understandings, or agreements, whether written or oral.
